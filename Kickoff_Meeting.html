Kick-Off Meeting - SustAInability

Kick-Off Meeting - 21st November 2024

Welcome to the Kick-Off Meeting of the SustAInability project! This meeting marks the official beginning of our initiative to integrate AI and sustainability into educational and environmental management practices.

The Kick-Off Meeting is a fantastic opportunity for students to get involved in international collaboration. During this meeting, we will discuss the formation of working groups and the topics they will address throughout the project. It's a great chance to experience international cultural exchange while learning from experts in environmental sciences and artificial intelligence.

Students will have the opportunity to meet with academics specializing in environmental sciences and AI, and work together to define the roadmap for our project. Guided by mentors, participants will work on specific topics, present their progress in upcoming workshops, and potentially contribute to academic publications.

The Kick-Off Meeting will also include an introduction session, where all participants will get to know each other, fostering a collaborative and friendly environment for the upcoming activities.

Date: 21st November 2024

Time: 10:00 - 13:00 (CET)
