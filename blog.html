Interview with Dr. Puthearath Chan

Dr. Puthearath Chan is a senior sustainability researcher at Cambodia’s Ministry of Environment and an adjunct lecturer at Paragon International University. In this interview, he highlights Cambodia’s cultural richness, evolving economy, and infrastructure advancements—such as new expressways—while addressing challenges like energy demands and climate change impacts along the Mekong River. He also emphasizes the government’s commitment to renewable energy, avoiding large hydropower projects on the Mekong, and the growing role of AI in environmental monitoring and sustainable urban development.
