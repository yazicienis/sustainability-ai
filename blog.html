About This Blog

Welcome to the SustAInability blog, where we explore the intersection of artificial intelligence, sustainability, and global collaboration. Through interviews and updates, we aim to highlight innovative research, best practices, and success stories from experts and students across multiple universities and countries. Join us as we share diverse perspectives on how AI can shape a more sustainable future.

A special thanks to Katharina Krohm, an SRH University Heidelberg student, for conducting this engaging conversations.
