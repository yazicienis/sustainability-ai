Embedded Systems for Sustainability

About the Study Group

The Embedded Systems for Sustainability group focuses on designing embedded systems using microcontrollers and sensors to collect environmental data and transmit it to the cloud. This initiative aims to leverage technology for sustainable development, offering real-time insights into various ecological metrics.

Project Goals

We plan to develop a long-term project that involves:

  • Designing and implementing robust embedded systems capable of operating in diverse environmental conditions.
  • Collecting high-quality data on environmental parameters.
  • Utilizing cloud technologies to store and analyze collected data, enhancing our understanding of environmental patterns.
