Summer School 2026 – Phnom Penh

AI, Sustainability & Cultural Exchange in Cambodia

About the Program

The SustAInability Summer School 2026 takes place in Phnom Penh, Cambodia, hosted by our partner Paragon International University. This one-week program brings together students from SRH University Heidelberg (Germany) and the University of Central Asia (Kyrgyzstan) for hands-on workshops, expert talks, and cultural exploration.

The program combines academic content on AI and sustainability with an unforgettable cultural experience — including a trip to the ancient temples of Angkor Wat.

Key Facts

  • Dates: August 2–8, 2026
  • Location: Paragon International University, Phnom Penh, Cambodia
  • Participants: 4 students from SRH University Heidelberg + 4 students from the University of Central Asia
  • Application Deadline: April 1, 2026

Program Highlights

  • Workshops in Phnom Penh: Hands-on sessions on AI, sustainability, and technology — hosted at Paragon International University.
  • Expert Talks: Presentations and discussions with local researchers, practitioners, and innovators.
  • Cultural Program: City tour of Phnom Penh including the Royal Palace, National Museum, and Wat Phnom.
  • Excursion to Siem Reap: Trip to the iconic temples of Angkor Wat — a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Intercultural Exchange: Daily interaction and collaborative work between students from Germany and Kyrgyzstan.
