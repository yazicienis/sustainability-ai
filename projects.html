Mentoring Group 1: AI for Environmental Monitoring

Focus Area: Leveraging AI technologies to monitor and manage environmental resources.

Students from Germany, Cambodia, and Kyrgyzstan are working together to develop AI-driven solutions to monitor deforestation, predict water scarcity, and assess air quality. Regular online meetings are held to discuss progress and exchange ideas, fostering both technological learning and intercultural understanding.

Mentoring Group 2: Sustainable Agriculture Using AI

Focus Area: Using AI to improve agricultural practices and ensure sustainability.

This group is focusing on how AI can be used to optimize agricultural output while reducing environmental impact. They aim to develop models that predict crop yields, optimize irrigation, and manage soil health. The participants are sharing knowledge from their respective cultures, enhancing both the technical and cultural depth of their project.

Mentoring Group 3: AI and Renewable Energy Solutions

Focus Area: Exploring AI's role in optimizing renewable energy systems.

Members of this group are investigating how AI can enhance renewable energy technologies, such as solar and wind energy. They are developing predictive models to optimize energy generation and storage. This collaboration encourages students to learn about renewable energy while appreciating each other's cultural perspectives.
