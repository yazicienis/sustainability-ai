Embedded Systems for Sustainability

This study group focuses on the design and implementation of embedded systems using microcontrollers and sensors to collect and transmit environmental data to the cloud. The project aims to advance sustainable development by providing real-time ecological insights and promoting long-term environmental monitoring and research.

Project TreeStrike

An autonomous drone-based reforestation system leveraging AI and advanced imaging to identify optimal planting sites. Integrating real-time weather data, offline capabilities, and precision drone navigation to maximize environmental restoration and sustainable land management.
