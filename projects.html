Projects

Short, focused overviews of our active project lines. Each card links to a dedicated page with setup guides, datasets, and teaching materials.

Global IoT Monitoring Network

LoRa-based environmental stations (solar, low-power) for temperature, humidity, air quality, and soil moisture across Heidelberg, Phnom Penh, and Naryn.

  • Open hardware & firmware; reproducible field kits
  • Data pipeline: gateway → CSV/DB → lightweight dashboards
  • Use in student labs, class projects, and community pilots

AI & Data for Climate Action

ML for forecasting and anomaly detection on sensor time series; energy optimization studies with open datasets.

  • Feature engineering & baselines (AR/ARIMA, regression)
  • Lightweight neural models for on-device/edge inference
  • Transparent evaluation & reproducible notebooks

Quantum Technologies for Sustainability

Intro modules and toy problems for optimization & simulation; hybrid QC/AI thinking for future-ready curricula.

  • Small-scale demos (variational optimization, scheduling)
  • Conceptual bridges to energy systems & materials topics
  • Teaching-first: simple APIs, clear learning outcomes
