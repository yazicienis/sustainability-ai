We are pleased to invite you to our upcoming two-day online workshop as part of the Sustainability and AI Project. Workshop Themes:
During the workshop, we will delve into various topics, such as:

  • AI-driven environmental monitoring and data analysis
  • Climate change physics and less-researched influences in climate systems
  • Renewable energy optimization (e.g., solar panel maintenance with reinforcement learning)
  • Resource management, composting applications, and sustainability solutions
  • Strengthening project groups and intercultural collaboration

Sample Talks & Project Ideas

Below are a few early submissions we have received so far:

  • Climate Physics: An introductory talk on the fundamental physics behind climate change.
  • The CompostLink Project: A mobile application designed to bridge the gap between food waste generators (restaurants, hotels, apartment complexes) and compost facilities, with the goal of repurposing food waste into valuable resources.
  • Understudied Influences in Climate Systems: A proposal focusing on cloud feedback loops, atomic vibrations, water vapor behavior, and the classification of different cloud types â€” addressing lesser-known factors in global warming.
  • Optimizing PV Panel Maintenance with Reinforcement Learning for Sustainable Solar Energy: A reinforcement learning-based approach to determine optimal cleaning schedules for PV panels, factoring in real-time environmental sensor data to improve energy output and sustainability.
