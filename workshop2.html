About the Workshop

This second online workshop in the SustAInability series brings together participants from environmental sciences, geography and GIS, water and climate-related studies, urban and regional planning, electrical and computer engineering, and IT/AI. The focus is on concrete applications of artificial intelligence for environmental monitoring, analysis of climate and land-use data, sensor networks and remote sensing, and sustainable management of water and other resources in urban and rural systems.

Over two mornings, students, lecturers, and practitioners will share short presentations, case studies, and demonstrations showing how AI tools can support decision-making in environmental and urban contexts, as well as in STEM and sustainability education.
