Research Scholarship — Cambodia 2026

3-month funded research stay at Paragon International University, Phnom Penh

About the Scholarship

As part of the SustAInability project, we are offering two fully funded research scholarships for students from SRH University Heidelberg to conduct a 3-month research stay at our partner university Paragon International University in Phnom Penh, Cambodia.

This is a unique opportunity to conduct hands-on international research, experience life in Southeast Asia, and contribute to our cross-cultural sustainability and AI research network.

Key Facts

  • Duration: 3 months
  • Period: Summer 2026 — stay must be completed by December 2026
  • Host: Paragon International University, Phnom Penh, Cambodia
  • Number of scholarships: 2
  • Monthly stipend: €1,225
  • Travel allowance: €1,500 (round-trip flight)
  • Total funding per scholarship: up to €5,175

Eligible Fields of Study

Applications are welcome from students in the following disciplines:

  • Artificial Intelligence / Computer Science / IT
  • Environmental Sciences
  • Social Sciences
  • Natural Sciences

Who Can Apply?

  • Currently enrolled undergraduate or Master's students at SRH University Heidelberg
  • Strong interest in international research and intercultural exchange
  • Good command of English (working language of the program)
