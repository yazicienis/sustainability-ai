SustAInability Summer School 2025

Empowering Sustainable Solutions through AI & Cultural Exchange

About the Summer School

Join our 14-day international summer school in Heidelberg to explore sustainability, AI, sensor networks, and cross-cultural collaboration. Participants will co-develop Environmental Data Monitoring Systems, engage in hands-on workshops, and take part in exciting excursions.

Program Highlights

  • Workshops on AI/ML, Embedded Systems, and Sensor Design
  • Mentorship by Professors and Engineering Students
  • 2-day Excursion to Taschachhaus
  • City tour of Heidelberg and cultural exchange activities
  • Open-air Poster Presentation & Picnic Dialogue

Who Can Apply?

Students from German universities, especially in engineering, AI, or sustainability fields. English proficiency (min. B2) and motivation for international teamwork are required.

What is Included?

  • Meals, excursions, technical materials
  • Certificate of Participation
