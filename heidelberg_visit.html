Heidelberg Visit — SustAInability Summer School 2025

27 July – 09 August 2025 • SRH Heidelberg • Excursion to the Alps • Mixed academic + cultural program

Overview

The two-week Heidelberg program blended hands-on workshops with cultural immersion. We built real prototypes (IoT sensor nodes, web dashboards, AI pipelines), explored quantum technologies, and experienced Heidelberg’s history and landscapes together. The summer school was highly productive: new project ideas emerged, long-term collaborations were initiated, and cross-cultural friendships formed.
