AI for Climate Change Resilience

AI-Driven Solutions for Climate Resilience and Sustainability

AI for Climate Change Resilience study group will explore cutting-edge applications of artificial intelligence to address critical challenges posed by climate change.

Focus Areas:

  • Climate Change Forecasting and Observation
    • Climate Modeling and Forecasting: AI application in enhancing climate change and models, or to forecast temperature variations and frequency of intense weather conditions, as well as precipitation.
    • Carbon Emission Tracking: Greenhouse gas emission management and visualization through satellite data and IoT sensors with the help of AI.
  • Solar and Wind Energy Efficiency
    • Dynamic Solar Panel Optimization: The use of AI techniques to optimize the angles and formation of the panels towards the sun intensity.
    • Wind Turbine Performance: A real-time artificial intelligence control to enhance the operation of wind turbine under dynamic conditions.
    • Resilience to Climate Extremes: The use of AI technologies that can enhance the site and operating life of the solar and wind farms during hard weather conditions such as hurricanes or head seasons.
  • Urban Sustainability
    • Smart Cities: AI-based solutions of urban heat islands, smart management of public transport and proper disposal of waste.
    • Building Energy Efficiency: AI in smart building solutions and future in development of smart buildings and integrating energy efficient solutions.
  • Agriculture
    • Precision Agriculture: AI-based strategies for predicting soil conditions, assessing of yield levels, and predicting water use under climate change conditions.
    • Flood Management: AI-based structures and frameworks for watering that are adaptable, and therefore prevent the losses of the crops.

This initiative aims to foster interdisciplinary collaboration and develop innovative AI-driven solutions to enhance resilience and sustainability in the face of climate change. Join us as we work towards a sustainable future!
