AI for Climate Change Resilience

AI-Driven Solutions for Climate Resilience and Sustainability

AI for Climate Change Resilience study group is under the leadership of Dr. Ayman Aljarbouh . This group will explore cutting-edge applications of artificial intelligence to address critical challenges posed by climate change.

Focus Areas:

  • Climate Change Forecasting and Observation: Leveraging AI for enhanced climate modeling, temperature variation forecasting, and greenhouse gas tracking through satellite data and IoT sensors.
  • Solar and Wind Energy Efficiency: Employing AI to optimize renewable energy systems, from solar panel orientation to wind turbine performance, while increasing resilience to extreme weather conditions.
  • Urban Sustainability: Promoting smart cities with AI solutions for energy-efficient buildings, waste management, and mitigation of urban heat islands.
  • Agriculture: Innovating precision agriculture strategies and AI-driven frameworks to manage water use, improve yields, and prevent crop losses due to floods.

This initiative aims to foster interdisciplinary collaboration and develop innovative AI-driven solutions to enhance resilience and sustainability in the face of climate change. Join us as we work towards a sustainable future!
