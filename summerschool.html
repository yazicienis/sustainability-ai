Summer School 2025 - Heidelberg

AI, Sustainability, and Cultural Exchange

About the Program

The Summer School 2025 at SRH University Heidelberg is designed for students in Germany to explore the dynamic intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Sustainability. The program includes workshops, excursions, technical visits, and intercultural activities.

Location

SRH University Heidelberg, Germany

Confirmed Program Dates

  • July 27, 2025: Arrival of participants in Heidelberg
  • July 28–29, 2025: Excursion to Taschachhaus (Alpine climate observation)
  • July 30, 2025: Return from the excursion
  • July 31, 2025: Welcome Day & Campus Tour (with SRH International Office)
  • August 8, 2025: Closing Dinner

Additional Program Activities (July 31 – August 8, 2025)

  • Workshops: Sessions on AI, IoT, Embedded Systems, and Sustainability. Includes hands-on group projects and student presentations.
  • Site Visit: Research Lab at TSG Hoffenheim – exploring AI in sports technology.
  • Startup & Innovation Visit: Guided tour of SRH Gründer Institute and introduction to the startup ecosystem.
  • Cultural Program: Excursion to Heidelberg Castle and local city tour.
  • Open-air Poster Presentation & Picnic Dialogue
  • Reflection and Closing: Group presentations, feedback session, and certificate ceremony.

Application Requirements

  • Curriculum Vitae (CV) with grade overview
  • Open to students currently enrolled in Heidelberg
