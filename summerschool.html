Summer School 2025 - Heidelberg

AI, Sustainability, and Cultural Exchange

About the Program

Our Summer School 2025 in Heidelberg brings together students from around the world to explore the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Sustainability. The two-week program includes workshops on machine learning, embedded systems, IoT, and prototype development, as well as cultural activities and a field trip to the Alps with experts from the German Weather Service (DWD).
